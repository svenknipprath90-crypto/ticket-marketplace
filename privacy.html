Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024

1. Information We Collect

We only collect data necessary to operate the Pi Ticket Marketplace. This includes your Pi Network username (provided securely via the Pi SDK), your shipping address (only if you choose to provide one for physical tickets), and transaction details related to your ticket purchases and sales.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to facilitate peer-to-peer ticket transactions, manage the escrow payout system, and provide customer support (e.g., chat functionality). We do not use your data for targeted advertising.

3. Data Sharing

Your Pi username is visible to the buyer/seller you transact with to ensure transparency. Your shipping address is only shared with the seller of the ticket you purchased. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.

4. Security

All data is securely stored using Supabase (PostgreSQL) with strict Row Level Security configurations. Payments are processed entirely by the official Pi Network SDK. We never have access to your Pi Wallet passphrase.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request data deletion, please contact the administrator (Gkcity1990) via the platform.
